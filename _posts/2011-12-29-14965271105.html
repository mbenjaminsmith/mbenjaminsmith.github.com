Here we’re testing basic collision detection as well as basic particle effects for projectiles and explosions.

The turret doesn’t track correctly because it’s assuming a circular tracking area. Tracking is better modeled with an ellipse (our foreshortened circle in our 2:1 perspective). This will be corrected.
